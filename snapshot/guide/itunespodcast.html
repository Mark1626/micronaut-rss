3 Itunes Podcast RSS Feed

Version: 1.0.1.BUILD-SNAPSHOT

3 Itunes Podcast RSS Feed

compile 'io.micronaut.configuration:micronaut-itunespodcast'
<dependency>
    <groupId>io.micronaut.configuration</groupId>
    <artifactId>micronaut-itunespodcast</artifactId>
</dependency>

An Itunes podcast feed, is a valid RSS 2.0 feed with additional tags.
